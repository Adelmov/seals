Join Jr-SCLA

5 W's

Why: Joining Jr-SCLA will help you getting started with coding.

Where: Class of Jr SCLA will take place on Zoom.

Who: students of color in middle School.

What: You will be part of amazing community of boys that will help you in challenges and help you grow as a leader.

When: The program starts on March 31st, 2021 and it ends on May 8th , 2021. Classes are from Wednesday, 5pm - 8pm & Saturday, 10am - 3:30pm.

How: You can filled your application in the TXT website.

Benefits

You will be able to learn, Javascript, CSS and HTML.

Increase your coding and leadership skills.

Help your community by solving a issue that they face.

You will have the opportunity to talk with panels of different profressionals from: Snapchat, Disney, Riot Games, and Google.

Learn how to manage your time.

Conclusion

In conclusion Jr-SCLA is a 8 week long program for boys of color in middle school. The program takes place via Zoom on March 31st, 2021 and it ends on May 8th, 2021. Participants will be able to reiceve a laptop during the program time. Teams will create a solution to a problem that their community is facing. In the Jr-SCLA program you will develop your coding and leadership skills.
